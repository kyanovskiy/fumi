M-THEORY

SHARING YOUR EXPERIENCE

M-Theory is a smartphone application that allows users to understand commcier cannabis products including their chemical and scientific data of cannabis products, and then crowdshare experiences, results and medical symptoms.

M-Theory also interfaces with StringTheory and allows dispensaries to review and shared crowdshared information about cannabis strains with their customers. M-Theory users have the option of using the app anonymously, or of creating an account that may be tied to their dispensary purchases to easily track their own experiences.

STRING THEORY

MANAGING YOUR EXPERIENCE

StringTheory is the most flexible platform available in the cannabis industry designed to make state compliance easy and reliable. Whether your business focuses on growing, manufacturing, dispensing or delivery, StringTheory’s adaptable technology allows you to easily collect manage and transfer cannabis information and generate tracking and filing reports required under state law.

StringTheory’s modular design interfaces with your existing software, and adapts to your specific requirements, meaning you only pay for what you need. StringTheory also gives you access to up-to-date changes in compliance, state by state. Simply select your industry type (grower, manufacturer, delivery, dispensary, etc.) and include your [license], and StringTheory gives you simple step-by-step instructions that make your tracking and compliance error and stress-free.

WHY IT IS IMPORTANT

Unlike pharmaceuticals, which have benefitted from decades and even centuries of medical research, cannabis products remain largely untested and often misunderstood by both consumers and medical professionals. M-Theory and StringTheory bridge the gap between use and collective knowledge, combining patients’ and users’ real world experience with science, and giving the medical profession access to anonymized data for research.
